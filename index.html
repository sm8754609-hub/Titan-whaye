How should I store my protein powder in Pakistan's climate?

Pakistan's varying climate requires specific storage considerations:

Humidity Control:

Store in airtight containers with silica gel packets to prevent clumping

Temperature:

Keep below 25°C, away from direct sunlight and heat sources

Summer Storage:

During extreme heat, consider refrigeration (not freezing)

Monsoon Precautions:

Use vacuum-sealed containers during humid months

Shelf Life:

Unopened: 24 months | Opened: 3-4 months with proper storage

Our packaging includes moisture-resistant barriers specifically designed for Pakistani conditions.
